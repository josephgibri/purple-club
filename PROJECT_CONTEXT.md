# Purple Club: Strategic Overview

## The Mission
Purple Club is a decentralized Merchant Discount Network. It is a "Social Layer" utility for the Purple Bitcoin (PBTC) community (a Community Takeover / CTO project). 

## The Business Logic
1. **The Membership Key:** Holding exactly 1 PBTC or more is the only requirement for access. 
2. **The "Handshake" Model:** We are NOT a payment processor. We connect users to merchants. Payments happen directly between them (Cash/Card/Crypto).
3. **Merchant Value Prop:** Merchants get free exposure to a high-net-worth crypto community in exchange for providing an exclusive, significant discount.
4. **Anchor Utility:** "Purple Travel" is our flagship internal merchant, providing global hotel discounts and driving the PBTC "Buy and Burn" mechanic.

## User Personas
- **The Holder:** Wants to save money on real-world expenses (Gyms, Coffee, Travel) because they hold PBTC.
- **The Merchant:** Wants a simple way to verify "Purple Club" members without complicated tech integrations.
